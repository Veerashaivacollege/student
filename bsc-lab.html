Veerashaiva College - B.Sc. Labs

Zoology Lab

The Zoology lab is equipped with modern tools and equipment to study animal biology and conduct various experiments related to animal physiology, anatomy, and taxonomy.

Botany Lab

The Botany lab provides facilities for the study of plant biology. Students can explore plant physiology, genetics, and ecology through various hands-on experiments.

Chemistry Lab

The Chemistry lab is well-equipped for conducting experiments in organic, inorganic, and physical chemistry. Students can learn about chemical reactions, molecular structures, and more.

Bio-Technology Lab

The Biotechnology Lab at Veerashaiva College is a state-of-the-art facility designed to support cutting-edge research and hands-on learning. It is equipped with advanced instruments and technologies that enable students and researchers to conduct experiments in various areas of biotechnology.

Physics Lab

The Physics lab is designed to facilitate experiments in classical and modern physics. Students can engage in experiments related to mechanics, electromagnetism, optics, and quantum physics.

Mathematics Lab

The Mathematics lab offers a conducive environment for learning and exploring mathematical concepts. Students can use various tools and resources to solve complex problems and understand theoretical principles.
