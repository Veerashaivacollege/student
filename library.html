About Our Library

The Veerashaiva College Library is a vast repository of knowledge, housing an impressive collection of 27,000 books across various disciplines. Established with the aim of supporting the academic and research needs of students and faculty, the library offers a diverse range of resources including textbooks, reference materials, journals, and digital media. The library is equipped with modern facilities such as computer workstations, high-speed internet access, and comfortable reading areas to provide an ideal environment for study and research. Additionally, the library features specialized sections for different fields of study, ensuring that students can easily access the materials relevant to their courses. With a dedicated staff always ready to assist, the Veerashaiva College Library is committed to fostering a culture of learning and intellectual growth. Regular workshops and seminars on information literacy and research methodologies are also conducted to enhance the skills of the library users.
